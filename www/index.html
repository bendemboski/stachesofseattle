'Staches of Seattle

A boy, his wife, their friend, a giant mustache, and the city they love

Well, Movember is nearly upon us, and I just so happen to have a giant mustache that I built for Halloween, a brilliant wife with a great idea for a unique way to participate in Movember, and an awesome photographer friend to help me pull it off.

Movember is a fundraiser where men grow mustaches throughout the month of November to raise awareness and funds for men's health issues. This year I'm going to help the city of Seattle participate in Movember by capturing 12 photos of my giant mustache on various Seattle landmarks throughout November, and using them to print and sell 2013 wall calendars, with the proceeds benefiting Movember. Think the Fremont troll, the Jimi Hendrix statue, the Space Needle, etc.

So, buy a calendar! Buy a few and give them as gifts! Or, if you love the idea, but already have enough wall calendars of famous landmarks sporting mustaches, then just donate to the cause!

I am selling professionally printed 8.5" x 11" (11" x 17" open) wall calendars with a picture of a different mustachioed Seattle landmark for each month for $15 apiece. where does the money go?

Having it shipped to you will cost you an extra $5, but I will announce several dates and times when you can pick up your calendar in downtown or North Seattle and save the $5 if you are local. The calendars will be printed and shipped or ready to be picked up the first week of December, in plenty of time for the holidays.

The good people at PayPal are standing ready and eager to help you support this cause however you see fit:

calendar(s) and have them shipped to you ($15 + $5 shipping)
calendar(s) and pick them up yourself ($15, no shipping)
to the cause because you're generous and awesome like that
